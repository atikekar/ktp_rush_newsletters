It's that time of year again! Rush Kappa Theta Pi, the nation's first and largest professional technology fraternity, to join a close-knit community of students passionate about tech, gain access to exclusive professional mentorship, and connect with our diverse alumni network.


WHAT DO WE DO?
Founded as the first Kappa Theta Pi chapter, we're dedicated to uniting students across the nation for the love of technology. KTP offers brothers the support to be extraordinary during their time at Michigan with resources centered around our five pillars: professional development, alumni connections, social growth, technological advancement, and academic support. From project teams and study groups to professional development workshops and hackathons / design jams, we foster a culture of growth encouraging members to pursue their tech passions.

WHO CAN APPLY?
Anyone with an interest in tech! We gladly accept (and encourage) rushees from all disciplines. The only requirement is that you must have at least 4 semesters left at the University of Michigan (including this semester).
